ABOUT THE EVENT

Annual Sports Events 2026 — a celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and the fighting spirit of PIEMR.

🏟️
About the Event

Annual Sports Events 2026 is organised by the Sports Department of Prestige Institute of Engineering Management and Research, Indore. Running 23–29 March 2026, the event brings together students across all branches to compete in 10 diverse sports.

🎯
Objective

To foster sportsmanship, teamwork, and physical fitness among students. A platform to showcase athletic talent, build camaraderie across branches, and enjoy a competitive yet friendly break from academics.

📋
Eligibility

All currently enrolled PIEMR students from 1st to 4th year across all branches. Each student may register for up to 2 sport events. Registration is free of cost.

🏆
Prizes

Winners and runners-up of each event will receive trophies and certificates. The best performer overall will be honoured with the Champion of Champions title at the closing ceremony.

Rules & Guidelines
PARTICIPATION RULES
  • Carry your College ID card at all times during the event.
  • Each student can register for a maximum of 2 sports events.
  • Participants must report to the venue 15 minutes before scheduled time.
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the referee / umpire is final and binding.
  • Team sports require a minimum number of registered members to participate.
  • T-shirts / jerseys provided must be worn during the event.
  • Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis. Slots are limited.
  • In case of bad weather, schedule may be adjusted by the organising committee.
  • For queries, contact the Sports Department between 9 AM – 4 PM on working days.
